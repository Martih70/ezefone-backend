Ezefone — Quick Guide
Everything you need, nothing you don't
1. Making a call from My Favourites
1Open Ezefone — you'll see your My Favourites screen
2Find the name of the person you want to call
3Tap their card once — your phone will dial them straight away
2. Adding or removing a favourite
1Tap the Contacts tab at the bottom
2Find the person in your list
3Tap the star ☆ to add them — it turns gold ★
4Tap the gold star ★ again to remove them
3. Adding a photo to a favourite card
1Tap the Contacts tab at the bottom
2Find the person — a blue camera button appears next to their name if they are one of your four favourites
3Tap the camera button and choose a photo — it will appear on their card straight away
To remove a photo: tap the grey remove-photo button next to the camera
4a. Adding a contact — Android
1Tap the Contacts tab at the bottom
2Tap Import from Phone to pick several contacts from your address book at once
Or tap Add Contact Manually to add one person at a time
4b. Adding a contact — iPhone
1Tap the Contacts tab at the bottom
2Tap Add Contact Manually
3Type their name and phone number, then tap Save
5. Sending a WhatsApp message
1Tap the WhatsApp tab at the bottom
2Find the person you want to message
3Tap the WhatsApp button next to their name
WhatsApp must be installed on your phone for this to work
6. Emergency SOS call
The red SOS button is always visible on the My Favourites screen.
Tap it once to call your emergency contact immediately.
To set your emergency contact: go to Settings → Emergency Contact (SOS)
7. If something goes wrong
1Close Ezefone completely
2Wait 5 seconds, then open it again
3If still not working, turn your phone off and back on
