1. Making a call
1Open Ezefone — you’ll see your My Favourites screen
2Find the name of the person you want to call
3Tap their card once — your phone dials them straight away
4When finished, tap the red button to hang up
2. Calling someone you rang recently
1After any call, a Recently Called strip appears above your favourite cards
2Tap any face in the strip to call them again instantly
Scroll left or right to see your last 8 calls
3. Adding or removing a favourite
1Tap the Contacts tab at the bottom
2Find the person and tap their row
3Tap Add to My Favourites — their card appears on your home screen
4To remove: tap their row again, then tap Remove from My Favourites
You can have up to 4 favourite cards
4. Setting a relationship label
1Tap the Contacts tab, then tap the person’s row
2Tap Set Relationship Label
3Type a short reminder — e.g. My Daughter or GP Surgery — then tap Save
The label appears below their name on the favourite card
5. Adding a new contact
1Tap the Contacts tab at the bottom
2Android: tap Import from Phone to bring in several contacts at once
3iPhone: tap Add Contact Manually, enter their name and number, tap Save
6. Adding a photo to a favourite
1Tap Contacts, then tap the row of a favourite contact
2Tap Change Photo and choose a picture from your phone
3The photo appears on their card straight away
To remove a photo: tap their row, then tap Remove Photo

7. Sending a WhatsApp message
1Tap the WhatsApp tab at the bottom
2Find the person you want to message
3Tap the WhatsApp button next to their name — WhatsApp will open ready to type
WhatsApp must be installed on your phone
8. Emergency SOS call
The red “Call for Help” button is always visible at the bottom of your home screen.

Tap it once to call your emergency contact immediately.

To set your emergency contact:
Settings → Emergency Contact (SOS)
9. If something goes wrong
1Screen frozen? Press the Home button, then tap Ezefone to reopen it
2Still wrong? Turn the phone off, wait 10 seconds, then turn it back on
3Cannot find the app? Open your browser and go to ezefone-backend-c8eadiyp.on-forge.com and sign in
4Battery low? Plug in your charger — a lightning bolt on screen means it is charging
Charge your phone every night so it is always ready
