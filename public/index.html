Help

MAKING CALLS
call
How do I call someone?
expand_more
1Tap the big card of the person you want to call
2Your phone will start ringing — wait for them to answer
3When finished, tap the red button to hang up
chat
How do I send a WhatsApp message?
expand_more
1Tap the WhatsApp tab at the bottom of the screen
2Find the person you want to message
3Tap the WhatsApp button next to their name — WhatsApp will open ready to message them
💡 WhatsApp must be installed on your phone for this to work
volume_up
I cannot hear anyone — what do I do?
expand_more
1Look at the right side of your phone
2Press the top button several times to raise the volume
💡 Keep pressing until the volume bar on screen is full
SOMETHING HAS GONE WRONG
refresh
The screen looks wrong or frozen
expand_more
1Do not worry — nothing is broken
2Press the round button at the bottom of your phone
3Find the Ezefone icon and tap it to open again
💡 Still wrong? Turn the phone off and back on — this fixes most problems
battery_alert
My phone is running out of battery
expand_more
1Plug your charger into the bottom of the phone
2Plug the other end into the wall socket
3A small lightning bolt appears — this means it is charging
💡 Charge your phone every night so it is always ready

USING THE APP
person_add
How do I add a contact? (Android)
expand_more
💡 Android — import several at once
1Tap Contacts at the bottom of the screen
2Tap Import from Phone to pick multiple contacts from your address book in one go
Or tap Add Contact Manually to add one person at a time
person_add
How do I add a contact? (iPhone)
expand_more
💡 iPhone — add one at a time
1Tap Contacts at the bottom of the screen
2Tap Add Contact Manually
3Type their name and phone number, then tap Save
star
How do I add or remove a favourite?
expand_more
1Tap Contacts at the bottom of the screen
2Find the person in your list
3Tap the star ☆ to add them — it turns gold ★ to show they are a favourite
4Tap the gold star ★ again to remove them from your favourites
💡 You can have up to 4 favourites shown as big cards on your home screen
add_photo_alternate
How do I add a photo to a favourite?
expand_more
1Tap Contacts at the bottom of the screen
2Find the person — a blue camera icon appears next to their name if they are one of your four favourites
3Tap the camera icon and choose a photo from your phone — it will appear on their card straight away
💡 To remove a photo, tap the grey remove-photo icon (next to the camera)
emergency
What is the red SOS button?
expand_more
1The red SOS button is for emergencies only
2Tapping it will immediately call your nominated emergency contact
3Set your emergency contact in Settings → Emergency Contact
💡 You can set this to a family member, carer, or 999
